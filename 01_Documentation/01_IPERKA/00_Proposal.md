# m431_ap24a_ForgottenLands

## The Product
### Forgotten Lands
* A game 2D RPG about a wanderer in the Forgotten Lands, solving Quests, fighting off monsters & heading for the finalboss, to free the lands.
* Features
  * Fight monsters
  * Explore areas
  * Collect items
  * Upgrade items
  * Complete quests
  * Save your progress
